Complete Line-by-Line Explanation of
Your Multi-Class Federated IDS Project
Overview of What Your Final System Does
Your project is a Federated Intrusion Detection System (IDS) using:
1. NSL-KDD dataset (5 classes)
2. Parallel CNN + LSTM model for multi-class attack detection
3. Federated Learning (FedAvg) with 3 clients
4. CrossEntropyLoss for multi-class training
5. Evaluation + visualization (accuracy, F1, confusion matrix)
Classes being predicted
Label encoder mapped 5 categories:
● Normal traffic
● DoS (Denial of Service)
● Probe
● R2L (Remote to Local)
● U2R (User to Root)

PART 6 — WHY PARALLEL CNN + LSTM?

CNN -> LSTM

Parallel new:
CNN || LSTM -> Fusion
Both learn independently.
CNN learns:
● spatial/local feature patterns
LSTM learns:
● dependencies
Fusion combines complementary signals.
Better.
